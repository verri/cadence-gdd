# System Synergies

The three combat systems are designed to feed each other:

- **Physical ↔ Magic cross-feed**: a fully completed Chain Strike grants a small **Active MP refund** at end of turn; an S-rank cast grants a small **Active HP refund**. Mixing modes pays off.
- **Defense → Long-term durability**: defending well keeps Active topped up, which feeds the **overflow → Reserve** mechanic (see [Rest & Recovery](battle/stats-and-resources.md#rest--recovery)). Skilled defenders passively heal Reserve over time without spending Rest turns.
- **Reading combos → Counter**: enemy combos open counter windows scaling with their length and Agility differential. A player who reads the boss correctly turns their most dangerous moments into the player's offensive openings (see [Chain Strikes](battle/chain-strikes.md), [Defense & Counter](battle/defense-and-counter.md)).
- **Magic → Defense**: a full Groove Meter (see [Rhythm Magic](battle/rhythm-magic.md#groove-meter-working-name)) can be spent to widen the next defense input window — letting mages convert magical excellence into defensive forgiveness.
- **Party play**: characters can pass momentum — when a character lands a perfect counter, the *next* party member to act gets a bonus to their input windows (an "Inspired" buff). Good play is contagious across the party.
- **Last Stand**: when *either* Reserve drops below 25%, the character enters desperation — Active gauge refreshes more aggressively that turn, giving them a real chance to claw back. The trade-off is structural: with Reserve already low, any overflow from over-spending lands directly on the dying pool. Playing aggressively at low Reserve is high-risk drama by default.

The intended emotional loop: **defend well → read the opening → counter hard → chain into a perfect cast → reset and do it better next round.**

## Character-Specific Synergies

Each party member amplifies a different part of the loop:

- **[Ren](characters/ren.md)** anchors the physical side — his long Chain Strikes create the most counter windows for enemies, but his completions feed MP cross-refund to offset his tiny magic pool.
- **[Sera](characters/sera.md)** converts magical excellence into survival via Mana Veil — her S-rank casts refund HP she doesn't need (it flows to Reserve overflow), and Groove Meter fills fast with her deep spell list.
- **[Kael](characters/kael.md)** is the counter specialist — he turns the "Reading combos → Counter" synergy into his primary damage source, and his perfect counters spread Inspired to the next party member.
- **[Lira](characters/lira.md)** pressures a different kill condition entirely (MP Reserve = 0), forcing enemies to choose between defending their HP and their MP — a dilemma that opens space for the rest of the party.
- **[Maren](characters/maren.md)** amplifies recovery mechanics — her Chain completions heal allies, her support spells extend Inspired and cleanse the status conditions that shut down recovery loops.
