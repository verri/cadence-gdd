# Design Pillars

1. **Every action is a performance.** No turn is a passive menu confirmation. Attacking, defending, and casting each have their own input minigame.
2. **Risk and reward through mastery.** Good timing isn't just optimal — it's transformative. A perfectly executed turn can swing a battle.
3. **Three rhythms, one battle.** Physical (combo timing), Defense (reactive timing), Magic (rhythm patterns) are mechanically distinct so the player switches mental modes constantly.
4. **Readable telegraphs.** Enemies and spells communicate clearly. Difficulty comes from execution, not guesswork.
