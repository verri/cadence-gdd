# Kael — The Counterpoint

A duelist built around reading and punishing enemy attacks. Extremely high Agility makes his input windows generous for both offense and defense. Balanced but modest pools mean he is not durable in a war of attrition — he wins by converting enemy turns into his own offense through perfect counters.

## Stats

| Stat | Value | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Vitality** | 50 | Average HP — decent but not deep |
| **Stamina** | 50 | Average recovery — sustainable but not aggressive |
| **Magic** | 40 | Below average MP — some magical options but not a caster |
| **Spirit** | 35 | Low MP recovery — magic is a secondary tool |
| **Agility** | 80 | Very high — wide counter windows, generous Chain timing |

## Magic Access

Limited but targeted. Magic is a secondary toolkit, not a primary strategy.

- **Elemental**: 2–3 spells — a speed buff, a debuff, one damage option. Utility, not firepower.
- **Psychic**: 1–2 spells — basic psychic attacks. Gives the party a way to pressure Veiled enemies when Lira isn't in the party.

## Mana Veil

**No innate Veil.** Can equip Partial Veil items if desired, but his balanced pools mean it's a genuine trade-off rather than a clear win.

## Playstyle

The "enemy turn" character. Kael's most important moments happen when the opponent attacks — he is always declaring counters, reading combo patterns, and converting defense into offense. His wide Agility windows make countering feel fluid rather than punishing. Offensively, his Chains are fast and have small counter windows (thanks to high Agility), making him hard to punish.

The trade-off: his moderate pools mean he cannot sustain a prolonged fight against heavy damage output. He needs to end fights efficiently or rely on Maren's recovery support.

*Riposte Cadence* is his signature design: a Chain that only unlocks after a perfect counter. The flow is counter → Riposte Cadence → the enemy's most dangerous turn becomes Kael's biggest damage turn.

**Distinct feel**: the player is most engaged during enemy turns when controlling Kael. His kit rewards *reading the opponent* over raw execution speed.

## Key Matchups

- **Strong against**: enemies with long wind-ups or multi-hit combos (huge counter windows at 80 Agility), slow heavy-hitters (his counter punish damage exceeds their single-hit investment), bosses with readable patterns
- **Weak against**: fast enemies with short attack animations (even 80 Agility can't widen a fundamentally tiny window), AoE-heavy enemies (counter isn't available vs AoEs), sustained damage over many rounds (his 50/50 pools bleed dry)
