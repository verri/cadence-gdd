# Maren — The Field Medic

A balanced character who keeps the party alive through recovery manipulation rather than raw healing spells. She interacts with the game's recovery mechanics (Active refresh, Reserve overflow, status conditions) in ways that amplify the party's sustain. Her personal combat ability is modest but well-rounded.

## Stats

| Stat | Value | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Vitality** | 55 | Slightly above average HP — can take some hits |
| **Stamina** | 60 | Good recovery — models her "sustainer" identity |
| **Magic** | 55 | Average MP — enough for support spells |
| **Spirit** | 55 | Average MP recovery — sustainable support casting |
| **Agility** | 40 | Below average — she relies on Guard more than Parry/Counter |

## Chain Strikes (3 slots)

| Chain | Hits | HP Cost | Notes | Found |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| *Pulse Strike* | 3 | 15 | Standard Chain. Completion triggers a small Active HP heal to one ally | Starting equipment |
| *Harmonic Drive* | 4 | 25 | Completion grants **Inspired** to the next party member to act (their next action refunds its Active cost on success) | Mid-game support-themed quest |
| *Cadence Break* | 5 | 35 | Completion removes one negative status condition from an ally | Late-game area with status-heavy enemies |

## Magic Access

Primarily support-oriented elemental magic, with unique recovery interactions.

- **Support spells**: party-wide Active refresh boost, status cleanse, temporary Partial Veil buff on an ally. These are her core contribution.
- **Elemental**: 2–3 damage spells (Tier 1–2), for when she needs to contribute damage directly.
- **Psychic**: 1 defensive psychic spell — reduces an enemy's Spirit, weakening their MP recovery. A soft counter to regenerating enemies.
- **Unique to Maren**: her S-rank casts on support spells have amplified effects (double duration, wider area). This rewards player skill at the rhythm minigame even on non-damage spells.

## Mana Veil

**No innate Veil.** Can equip Partial Veil items. Her balanced pools make Partial Veil a genuine tactical choice for specific encounters.

## Playstyle

Party enabler. Maren's turns are about reading the *party's* state — who needs Active HP, who has a dangerous status condition, who is about to act and would benefit from Inspired. She is the character that makes the rest of the party better. Her personal damage output is the lowest, but a party with Maren survives significantly longer than one without her.

Her Chain Strikes deliver support effects through execution, not through passive menus. *Pulse Strike* heals an ally on completion — so the player is still playing the timing minigame, and the quality of their execution determines whether the heal lands. *Cadence Break* cleanses a status condition, but only if the player completes the 5-hit chain without missing.

**Distinct feel**: the player is watching the party's health bars and status icons, not the enemy. Maren's decisions are about triage and timing — who to buff, when to cleanse, when to contribute damage instead.

## Key Matchups

- **Strong against**: status-heavy enemies (*Cadence Break* and cleanse spells directly counter Bleed/Burnout/Cursed), long attrition fights (her recovery manipulation extends the party's staying power), encounters that threaten multiple party members simultaneously
- **Weak against**: burst-heavy enemies (she can't heal fast enough if an ally is killed in one turn — she prevents attrition, not one-shots), psychic enemies (average MP pool, no special defense), encounters that demand high personal damage (she's the last pick for a race-the-clock fight)
