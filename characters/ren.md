# Ren — The Chain Artist

A swordsman who fights in continuous motion. High HP pool and strong HP recovery let him chain expensive combos back-to-back. His small MP pool is a structural vulnerability — psychic damage has a short path to killing him, and he has almost no magical options.

## Stats

| Stat | Value | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Vitality** | 85 | Large HP pool — can afford expensive Chains without touching Reserve |
| **Stamina** | 75 | Strong Active HP refresh — sustains aggressive combo play |
| **Magic** | 25 | Tiny MP pool — MP Reserve is dangerously shallow |
| **Spirit** | 20 | Minimal MP recovery — can't sustain casting, can't recover from MP damage |
| **Agility** | 45 | Slightly below average — moderate windows, not a counter specialist |

## Chain Strikes (5 slots)

| Chain | Hits | HP Cost | Notes | Found |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| *Twin Crescent* | 3 | 15 | Starter Chain — fast, safe, low commitment | Starting equipment |
| *Stormpiercer* | 5 | 40 | Bread-and-butter mid-weight Chain | Early-game quest reward |
| *Ironwake* | 6 | 55 | Heavy finisher. Branch input on hit 5: damage path vs. knockback path (unlocked at [Mastery 2](../progression/mastery-system.md)) | Smithing-themed dungeon |
| *Threadcutter* | 4 | 25 | Fast Chain that inflicts **Bleed** on completion | Boss drop — speed-type enemy |
| *Avalanche Rondo* | 7 | 75 | Signature move. Devastating but nearly suicidal at low Active HP. Huge counter windows | Late-game hidden area |

## Magic Access

Minimal. Can learn 1–2 basic elemental spells (Tier 1 only). His tiny MP pool means casting is an emergency option, not a strategy. No psychic access.

## Mana Veil

**No.** Equipment-based Partial Veil only — and probably not worth it. His small MP pool means Veil just moves the death clock to a shorter fuse.

## Playstyle

Aggressive attacker who must defend well to keep his HP economy healthy. Ideal against slow, heavy enemies (his moderate Agility gives decent windows against slower foes). Vulnerable to psychic damage and enemies that apply Burnout (can't recover from MP attrition). In a party, he needs someone who can draw psychic aggro or cleanse status conditions.

**Distinct feel**: the player spends most of Ren's turns in the Chain Strike minigame — long combo sequences with branch decisions. His turns are the longest and most physically engaging.

## Key Matchups

- **Strong against**: slow physical enemies (big counter windows, deep HP pool to absorb trades), Veiled mages (his physical damage goes through their HP if they drop Veil, or eats their MP through Veil)
- **Weak against**: psychic enemies (25 MP Reserve is 2–3 psychic hits from death), status-heavy enemies (Bleed + Burnout shuts down his HP recovery engine), high-Agility enemies (his 45 Agility means tight offensive windows)
