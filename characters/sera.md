# Sera — The Veiled Elementalist

A mage who uses her enormous MP pool as both a spell reservoir and a damage sponge via Mana Veil. She converts physical beatings into MP attrition, letting her survive in the front line far longer than her thin HP would suggest. But psychic damage is devastating — it drains the same pool she uses to cast and to survive.

## Stats

| Stat | Value | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Vitality** | 30 | Fragile HP pool — cannot take physical hits on HP |
| **Stamina** | 25 | Poor HP recovery — if the Veil drops, she's in immediate danger |
| **Magic** | 90 | Massive MP pool — deep casting reserves AND deep survival reserves |
| **Spirit** | 80 | Excellent MP recovery — sustains heavy casting and absorbs Veil damage |
| **Agility** | 25 | Low — tight input windows, punished for slow reactions |

## Magic Access

Broadest elemental access in the party. She is the primary spellcaster and can learn the most spells of any character.

- **Elemental**: full access — Fire, Frost, Lightning, Earth. Potentially 8–10 spells by endgame across all tiers (Tier 1 through Tier 3).
- **Psychic**: none. Her mind is structured for external elemental projection, not internal psychic manipulation.

## Mana Veil

**Yes, innate.** This is her core mechanic. Veil management defines her moment-to-moment play:

- **Veil up**: physical and elemental damage route to MP. Leverages her massive 90 Magic pool as armor. She can front-line against physical enemies indefinitely — as long as she has MP.
- **Veil down**: damage hits her fragile 30 Vitality HP. Necessary when MP Reserve is threatened or when psychic enemies are present.

The toggle creates a recurring tactical decision every turn: which pool can I afford to bleed from right now?

## Playstyle

A rhythm-game specialist. Her turns center on the casting minigame, where her broad spell list and high MP pool let her choose from many options. Defensively, she is unusual: she *wants* to take hits (through the Veil) rather than Parry them perfectly, because her MP recovery is so strong. But she benefits from good defense anyway — Guarding through the Veil means damage hits Active MP only, preserving Reserve.

Against psychic-heavy encounters, she must be protected or removed from the front line. Psychic damage bypasses the Veil and goes straight to the pool she uses for everything.

**Distinct feel**: the player is constantly toggling Veil, watching MP numbers, and performing rhythm minigames. Sera's turns feel like resource management layered on top of musical performance.

## Key Matchups

- **Strong against**: physical enemies (Veil converts their damage to MP attrition, which she recovers efficiently), elemental enemies (same Veil redirect), long attrition fights (her Spirit 80 recovery is relentless)
- **Weak against**: psychic enemies (hard counter — they bypass Veil and target her survival pool directly), high-Agility enemies (her 25 Agility means very tight casting and defense windows), Veil-dispel effects (if enemies can strip Veil, she's exposed at 30 HP)
