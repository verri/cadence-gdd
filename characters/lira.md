# Lira — The Psychonaut

A psychic who attacks the enemy's MP pool directly. In a world where MP Reserve = 0 means death, Lira threatens a kill condition that most enemies and players don't guard well. She is the hard counter to Veiled mages and shield-heavy enemies, but she is physically fragile and her own MP economy is tight (psychic powers are expensive).

## Stats

| Stat | Value | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Vitality** | 35 | Low HP — physically vulnerable |
| **Stamina** | 30 | Poor HP recovery — cannot sustain physical attrition |
| **Magic** | 70 | Good MP pool — needed for expensive psychic powers |
| **Spirit** | 65 | Good MP recovery — can sustain psychic casting over a long fight |
| **Agility** | 50 | Average — no particular advantage or disadvantage |

## Chain Strikes (3 slots)

| Chain | Hits | HP Cost | Notes | Found |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| *Nerve Strike* | 3 | 12 | Basic physical attack that routes a portion of damage to MP — a physical-psychic hybrid | Starting equipment |
| *Synapse Rend* | 4 | 25 | Physical combo that inflicts **Burnout** on completion (enemy Active MP stops refreshing) | Medical/anatomical dungeon |
| *Cognitive Fracture* | 5 | 35 | Heavy chain. Completion inflicts **Drained** on target (Spirit treated as halved) | Boss drop — psychic enemy |

## Magic Access

Psychic specialist with limited elemental fallback.

- **Psychic**: full access — 4–6 psychic spells by endgame (mind blast, dread, mental crush, sanity erosion, etc.). This is her primary damage school. All psychic damage routes to MP, bypassing Mana Veil.
- **Elemental**: 1–2 basic spells (Tier 1 only), for situations where psychic is irrelevant (enemies with very small MP pools where physical damage is faster).

If the "distinct psychic minigame" question from [Open Questions](../open-questions.md) is resolved, Lira is the character who engages with it most.

## Mana Veil

**No innate Veil.** Her MP pool is her offensive resource, not her defensive one. Routing damage into the pool she casts from would be self-defeating.

## Playstyle

Strategic attacker. Lira's value is not in raw DPS but in targeting the enemy's weakness — specifically, draining MP Reserves. Against enemies with Mana Veil or large MP pools (like mage-type bosses), she is devastating. Against purely physical enemies with tiny MP pools, her psychic damage has a short path to triggering the kill condition, but those enemies can also just beat her down physically faster than she can drain them.

Her Chain Strikes are designed to complement her casting: *Synapse Rend* inflicts Burnout (stopping enemy MP recovery) before she starts draining their MP with spells. *Cognitive Fracture* halves their Spirit, weakening their MP recovery permanently. The sequence is: debilitate recovery → drain the pool.

**Distinct feel**: the player thinks about the *enemy's* resource bars, not their own. Lira's turns involve choosing which enemy pool to pressure and when to apply status conditions that shut down recovery.

## Key Matchups

- **Strong against**: Veiled mages (psychic bypasses Veil, drains their survival pool), enemies with large MP pools (more to drain, and they're often caster-types who depend on that MP), regenerating enemies (Burnout + Drained shuts down recovery)
- **Weak against**: purely physical enemies (they hit HP, she has 35 Vitality; they may have tiny MP pools that aren't worth targeting), fast enemies (average Agility means no defensive edge), enemies immune to status conditions (her Chain Strike value depends on Burnout/Drained landing)
